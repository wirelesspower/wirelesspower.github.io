Introduction

This space is created to freely share knowledge and information under a Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Licence) on Wireless Power Trasnfer (WPT) systems. The site contains a collection of lectures, seminars and literature on WPT system along with example designs, simulation models, CAD models, etc.

The presentations are written in Markdown, enabling experts to easily contribute.

The Team

This site is maintained by the power electronics research group at the University of Auckland. The group is well known worlwide for thier cotributions to the field of WPT.
